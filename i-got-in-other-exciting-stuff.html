I got in !!!! ++ other exciting stuff

erikburk 30 August 2025

Tja tja blog erik is here with another post for yall

I have realized that there are quite a few (about one or two) non swedish speakers that also want to follow my blog. So erm yeah here it is in english n stuff. 

The big thing is that into Canada this time!!!!! I landed in Toronto and the border guards were way nicer than in Montreal. They glanced at my papers for a good five minutes, and then I was on my way. Have officially become a federalist. Long live the unfree and expensive quebec.

This time i flew there via Air Canada. Some remarks compared to KLM:

  • Comfier seats
  • Food was more or less same
  • Kinda sad to not be able to visit Amsterdam while waiting for the next flight.
  • They also served wine with the food. I didn't try it this time though.
  • There was a baby and a dog that took turns to scream and bark the entire flight. A dog ???? the flip ???

Also, some Toronto slander. It looks like the second city you build in Cities Skylines. It's all just copy+paste, hella large land area for no reason with cul de sacs as far as you can see. And then like 20 skyscrapers. Also like huge intersections and 5+ lane highway all over the place. Very cliché american city. Euro brain not like.

This week has been quite crazy. I was sick and just kinda depressed for the first few days after i came back. Also, the police met me at arlanda aswell 💀 like i couldnt just walk out of the airport in amsterdam never to return there. They just wanted to check that i came back and that was it basically. 3 police (policemen? but there was a policewoman too? policiers ? man english is silly) met me just to say hi. I am so happy to see that my taxes go to the right causes <3 anyways when i got better i could go back to uppsala to participate a bit in the engineer reception. Was very fun. Also ! I recieved my study permit, three days before departure !!! meaning that i can stay for the whole year if i want !!!

Then came disaster.

- Hello Erik you might have to pay 4000cad OR 12000cad since you were removed from canada last time. But idk if and which of them lol good luck have fun bbgirl, said the immigration responsible at Polytechnique.

Erm the flip ? i dont have that amount of money to just pay upon arrival

- I will look into it lol slay

So two nights before i was supposed to go, I didnt even know if had to pay crazy amounts of money to come back into the country. Yesterday night though i got a new email saying that she thinks it's very unlikely. So i went anyways (duh) and no sign of paymment so far. If i do have to pay though, idk what to do. 4000cad sure its doable but 12000 cad ? nej tack ill just flee back to sweden in that case

I am currently waiting for the Montreal flight. Kinda hungry but idk if i want to eat here or when i arrive. Poutine would be banger. But then id have to wait like 4 hours for food. A great predic. Anyways i got in and i have all the documents and everything. And i learnt the other day that monday 1/9 is labor day, meaning it's a day off. What even is that? And from what i understand, 1/5 is not a day off. Whats wrong with yanks and canucks bro. But anyways that means that i can move into the apartment in peace, with no classes that day. Hell yeah.

Other fun things:

  • There is some way to get my blog in an RSS feed. I can kinda get it to work on my computer RSS reader, with the first post missing, but not my phone. Also great rundown on what RSS is and why its amazing can be found here, with credit originally to maxs blog post on the matter.
  • Max also has a blog now! His website is https://maxedah.com and you should def go check him out aswell!
  • There is no comment features on my blog, but i have created a whatsapp group that should be open for anyone to join, with the intent of letting people comment there. I will also notify ppl about when i post there so that cring peopl without an rss reader can also get notifications.

anyways have a nice weekend everyone and yall better continue to read this blog now that my exchange has actually started >:) puss pusss
